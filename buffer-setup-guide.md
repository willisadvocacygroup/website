## HOW TO CONNECT EACH CHANNEL (2 minutes each)
1. Go to: https://publish.buffer.com
2. In left sidebar → click **Add Channel**
3. Select the channel type (LinkedIn / Instagram / Facebook)
4. You'll be redirected to that platform to authorize Buffer
5. Click **Authorize** / **Allow** when prompted
6. Return to Buffer — channel appears in sidebar

**LinkedIn:** Connects your personal profile OR a company page. Connect your personal profile (Uhia Willis) first — that's where the speaker content goes.

**Instagram:** Requires a Business or Creator account (not personal). If @uhiawillis is a personal account, switch it to Creator in Instagram Settings → Account → Switch to Professional Account.

**Facebook:** Connect Willis Advocacy Group page. You must be an admin of the page.
