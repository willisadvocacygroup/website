What This Is

The Mastermind is for men who have walked the framework, walked it again, and are now in the build phase of their own life's work — and who want to do that build in a room of peers, not above them or beneath them.

This is not a course. This is not a coaching program. This is a peer council with weight.

Iron sharpens iron. The point of the Mastermind is iron at altitude — not where it's comfortable, but where it has to be sharp to survive.

The Room

  • 10 men. Hand-vetted. Annual cohort.
  • 4 in-person gatherings/year — 3 days each, hosted in destination locations
  • Monthly virtual round-tables — strategy, accountability, deep work
  • Direct access to me at the level reserved for this room
  • Peer-to-peer build support — your fellow Mastermind members are the resource
  • One-on-one quarterly intensive with me — 90 minutes, fully dedicated
  • The Mastermind brotherhood — lifelong. The room continues after the year ends.

Who Belongs

You're a fit if you are —

  • A founder, operator, or leader running a business, ministry, organization, or platform with measurable scale
  • A speaker, author, or coach building a body of work that needs sharpening at this level
  • A man who has walked through real collapse — and is now in the build, not just the recovery
  • An Ambassador or Cohort graduate who is ready for peer-to-peer altitude
  • A man for whom the price is meaningful but not prohibitive — the room only works if everyone is fully in

Investment

Investment is discussed privately with qualified applicants only. This room is priced to reflect the level and the time commitment involved.
