This is not the Blueprint

The Rebuild Blueprint is the full self-paced system — seven modules, all seven phases, workbooks you work alone on your schedule. It's built for the man who moves best with autonomy.

The cohort is built for the man who is done going it alone. The curriculum is the same. The difference is what happens between the modules: live calls, a room full of men in the same fight, and Uhia with eyes on where you are in the process.

The framework works alone. It works faster when you're not.

A closed group with the other men in your cohort. This is not a public forum.
