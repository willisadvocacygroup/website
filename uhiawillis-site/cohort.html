What This Is

Once a quarter, I run a closed-door cohort of men working through the R.E.B.U.I.L.D. Method together.

This is not a course you watch alone. This is not a 500-person Facebook group that goes silent in week 3. This is the room.

10 to 15 men. Vetted. Application-required. Weekly live sessions. Direct accountability. Sharpening. No audience. No performance.

You only get one chance to walk a framework with the man who built it, alongside other men who showed up because they had to. This is that chance.

What's Inside

  • 12 weeks of live weekly sessions — 90 minutes each, on camera, on the framework, on each man's actual life
  • The full R.E.B.U.I.L.D. Blueprint included — all 7 modules, workbooks, integration assignments

Who Gets In

Application is mandatory. I read every one personally. I'm looking for:

  • A man who has walked through a real collapse — divorce, betrayal, business loss, reputation hit, addiction recovery, reentry, executive derailment, identity disruption from a role or system collapse
  • A man ready to do the work — not talk about doing the work
  • A man who can show up weekly, on camera, with what's actually happening in his life
  • A man who can hold confidence for the other men in the room
  • A man who knows that brotherhood is not free — it costs time, vulnerability, and showing up

If you can't commit to 90 minutes a week for 12 weeks, don't apply. This room only works if every man holds his post.

Investment

Cohort investment is sized to the room — not to the market. Discussed on the strategy call after your application is reviewed.

If you're applying for the right reasons, the price will not be the deciding factor. If price is your deciding factor, this is not the room for you.
