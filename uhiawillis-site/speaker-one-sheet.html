"You don't need a perfect past to build a powerful future."

Uhia Willis is a speaker, author, and founder of The R.E.B.U.I.L.D. Method — a 7-phase framework for men rebuilding identity, purpose, and leadership after life collapse. He built the framework from necessity, after living through the kind of collapse that strips away everything you thought defined you. The R.E.B.U.I.L.D. Method is the system he needed and couldn't find. He built it so other men don't have to figure it out alone.

He speaks to men's audiences, faith communities, leadership groups, and anyone who believes that the worst chapter of a man's life can become the foundation for his most purposeful one.
