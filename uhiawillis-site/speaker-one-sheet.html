Who Uhia Is

Uhia Willis is the founder of The R.E.B.U.I.L.D. Method™ — a 7-phase framework for men and leaders rebuilding after collapse. Forged from his own lived experience of marriage loss, attacks on his name, faith-leader abandonment, and the silent betrayal of his closest network, R.E.B.U.I.L.D. is the system he built in the wreckage of his own life.

Uhia speaks to two audiences with one message: the man on the floor, and the leader at altitude. The frameworks are the same. The construction sequence is the same. The discipline is the same.

He is a licensed broker, speaker, coach, and the architect of a movement that meets men where collapse has put them — and walks them, phase by phase, to where they were always supposed to be.
